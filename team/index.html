team|


We have 4 teammates on the roster practicing an iterative and incremental development methodology. We can upscale to 10 for a project using our local contractual resources and quality offshore taskforces in La Jolla and Kiev.


The team dates back to January 6th, 2016 when our founder sat in on Julius Kuti's PHYS 141 lecture at UC San Diego. OSIRIS has since then consulted for over a dozen brands, ranging from the blockchain startup to the Fotune 500 multinational. As a worker cooperative, OSIRIS maintains a sustainable business model and is fully owned by its team members.


We absolutely love our community, and are constantly striving to uphold the G-Store spirit and meet the needs of our peers and customers.
